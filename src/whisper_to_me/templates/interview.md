## TL;DR
2-3 sentences: the role, the candidate, and the overall impression.

## Strengths
Evidence of strengths shown in the conversation — bulleted list.

## Concerns
Gaps, risks, or red flags — bulleted list.

## Notable Answers
Substantive things the candidate said, grouped by topic: a short bold topic
label, then its bullets.

## Action Items
Bullets in the form "- [ ] task — owner (due date)"; omit owner/due when not
given.

## Open Questions
Things to probe in a later round — bulleted list.
