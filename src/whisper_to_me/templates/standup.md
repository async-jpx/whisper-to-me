## TL;DR
2-3 sentences on the team's overall state and momentum.

## Updates by Person
A short bold name label, then that person's progress bullets.

## Action Items
Bullets in the form "- [ ] task — owner (due date)"; omit owner/due when not
given.

## Blockers
Bulleted list of blockers and who is stuck on what.

## Open Questions
Bulleted list.
